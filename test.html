DRINKS AND SMOOTHIES

Our drinks and smoothies section!

STRAWBERRY BANANA


Strawberry Banana

RECIPE

Blend 1 banana, 1 cup strawberries, 1/2 cup each vanilla yogurt and milk, 2 teaspoons honey, a pinch of cinnamon and 1 cup ice.

STRAWBERRY SHORTCAKE


Strawberry Shortcake

RECIPE

Blend 2 cups strawberries, 1 cup crumbled pound cake, 1 1/2 cups each milk and ice, and sugar to taste. Top with whipped cream and more strawberries.

TRIPLE-BERRY BLEND


Triple-Berry

RECIPE

Blend 1 1/2 cups mixed blackberries, strawberries and raspberries with 1 cup each milk and ice, and sugar to taste.

RASPBERRY ORANGE


Raspberry-Orange

RECIPE

Blend 1 cup each orange juice and raspberries, 1/2 cup plain yogurt, 1 cup ice, and sugar to taste.

TANGEY PEACH MANGO BANANA


Tangey Peach-Mango-Banana

RECIPE

Blend 1 cup each chopped fresh or frozen peaches and mango, 1 cup each plain yogurt and ice, 1/2 banana, and sugar to taste.

HONEYDEW ALMOND


Honeydew-Almond

RECIPE

Blend 2 cups chopped honeydew melon, 1 cup each almond milk and ice, and honey to taste.

TANGERINE HONEY


Tangerine-Honey

RECIPE

Peel and seed 4 tangerines, then blend with the juice of 2 limes, 1/4 cup honey and 1 cup ice.

BLUEBERRY PEAR


Blueberry-Pear

RECIPE

Blend 1 1/2 cups frozen blueberries, 1 chopped pear, 1 1/2 cups each maple or plain yogurt and ice, and sugar to taste.

SWEET LIME TEA


Sweet Lime Tea

RECIPE

1 gallon boiling water, 6 black tea bags, 1 1/2 cups white sugar' and 4 limes jucied.

POMERGRANTE CHERRY


Pomegranate-Cherry

RECIPE

Blend 1 cup frozen pitted cherries, 3/4 cup pomegranate juice, 1/2 cup plain yogurt, 1 tablespoon honey, 1 teaspoon lemon juice, a pinch each of cinnamon and salt, and 2 cups ice.

CREAMY PINEAPPLE


Creamy Pineapple

RECIPE

Blend 2 cups chopped pineapple, 1/2 cup cottage cheese, 1/4 cup milk, 2 teaspoons honey, 1/4 teaspoon vanilla, a pinch each of nutmeg and salt, and 2 cups ice.

PINEAPPLE COCONUT FREEZE


Pineapple-Coconut

RECIPE

Freeze about 2 cups coconut water in 1 or 2 ice-cube trays. Blend 2 cups each chopped pineapple and coconut ice cubes, 1 1/2 tablespoons lime juice, 1 tablespoon honey and 1/2 cup coconut water.

BLACK RASPBERRY VANILLA TWIST


Black Raspberry–Vanilla:

RECIPE

Blend 1 pint blackberries, 1/2 cup raspberries, 1 cup vanilla yogurt and 1 tablespoon honey.

MANGO ACAI


Mango-Acai

RECIPE

Blend two 4-ounce packages frozen acai berry puree, 1 cup chopped mango, 1/2 cup orange juice and 2 cups ice.

PEACH GINGER


Peach Ginger

RECIPE

Blend 2 cups frozen sliced peaches, 1 1/2 cups buttermilk, 3 tablespoons brown sugar and 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger.

STRAWBERRY OATMEAL SMOOTHIE


Strawberry Oatmeal Breakfast Smootie

RECIPE

1 cup soy milk, 1/2 cup rolled oats, 1 banana, 14 frozen strawberries, 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla exstract, and 1 1/2 teaspoon white sugar.

PRETTY IN PINK SANGRIA


Pretty In Pink Sangria

RECIPE

2-10 onuce-packages frozen sliced strawberries, 1 can frozen lemonade, 2 bottles chilled rose wine, 2 cups pineapple jucie, and 1 liter ginger ale.

CREAMY HOT COCOA


Creamy Hot Cocoa

RECIPE

1/3 cup unsweet coca butter, 3/4 cup white sugar, 1 pinch salt, 1/3 cup boiling water, 3 1/2 cups milk, 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract, and 1/2 cup half and half cream.
